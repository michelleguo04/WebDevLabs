I am an Information Systems major sophomore undergraduate at Carnegie Mellon University. I have an interest in technology, AI, and data science. Growing up in a city driven by technological innovation, I developed an early fascination with the intersection of data, design, and real-world problem-solving. I was captivated by how technology shapes communities, enhances safety, and drives progress. This curiosity extended beyond academics - I found myself drawn to creative problem-solving through digital design, 3D modeling, and photography, analyzing how natural and human-made environments interact. Whether traveling to new places or simply walking through different neighborhoods, I became attuned to the patterns that shape the coexistence of people and nature.
